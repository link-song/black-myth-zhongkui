The Legend of Zhong Kui

Zhong Kui, the King of Ghosts and Demon Hunter in Chinese Mythology

In ancient Chinese mythology, Zhong Kui was a scholar who, despite his exceptional talent, was denied the top position in the imperial examinations due to his disfigured appearance. After his death, he became a powerful demon hunter, protecting the mortal world from evil spirits.

Black Myth: Zhong Kui reimagines this legendary tale, placing you in the role of this iconic character as you navigate a world where the boundaries between the mortal realm and the supernatural are blurred.
